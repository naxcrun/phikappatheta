Our Brotherhood

The Indiana Zeta Colony of Phi Kappa Theta at Purdue University is more than just a social organization. We are a brotherhood grounded in Catholic ideals, dedicated to the holistic development of our members through rigorous spiritual, academic, and leadership formation.

Spiritual Development

We take our faith seriously. Our chapter implements a multi-semester spiritual development program designed to help brothers grow deeper in their faith. Working closely with the St. Thomas Aquinas Catholic Center, we encourage active participation in mass, dedicated bible studies, and a vibrant community life that supports moral and intellectual growth.

Whether we are conceptualizing new spaces for our chapter, building custom tools for our brotherhood, or excelling in the classroom, the men of Indiana Zeta expect excellence from themselves and each other.
